ABOUT US

At Drilax, we deliver high-quality hardware tools designed for builders, engineers, and DIYers. We started as a small hardware shop in 2010, frustrated by the lack of good-quality tools in the market. That’s when we decided to build our own brand — one that focuses on quality, trust, and customer satisfaction.

From humble beginnings to becoming a recognized name in the hardware space, our journey has been driven by passion and persistence. With over 10 years of experience, we proudly supply products across Kerala and outside Kerala, serving both local and out-of-state clients.
